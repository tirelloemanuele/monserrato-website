About the Space

Nestled in the heart of Rome, this elegant apartment offers the perfect blend of historical charm and modern comfort. Located just steps away from iconic sites such as Piazza Navona, the Pantheon, and Campo de' Fiori, you'll find yourself immersed in the rich culture and vibrant energy of the Eternal City.

The apartment features beautiful wooden ceiling beams and warm, inviting decor with comfortable mustard yellow furniture. With two rooms, including a cozy bedroom with a double bed and a spacious living room with a comfortable sofa, this apartment can accommodate up to 4 guests. The fully equipped kitchen includes a refrigerator, stove, and oven for preparing meals during your stay.

The modern bathroom is clean and well-maintained, with all necessary amenities including a glass-enclosed shower, toilet, bidet, and sink. Towels and bed linens are included for your convenience. This is a non-smoking accommodation, perfect for a comfortable stay in the heart of Rome.
