How to get a shareable link (Drag & Drop method):
Open your web browser and go to app.netlify.com/drop.
Locate your project folder on your desktop: Monserrato website.
Drag and drop that entire folder right onto the web page where it says "Drag and drop your site folder here".
Wait a few seconds. Netlify will upload your files and give you a unique URL (like https://calm-seahorse-123456.netlify.app).
Copy that link and send it to anyone!
